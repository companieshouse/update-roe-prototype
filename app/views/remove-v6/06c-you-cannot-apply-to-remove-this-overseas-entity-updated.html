You cannot apply to remove this overseas entity

We cannot remove an overseas entity if it:

  • owns any relevant property or land in the UK
  • still appears on any land registry records as the registered owner of relevant property or land in the UK

What does 'relevant property or land' mean?

This means property or land in the UK that was bought on or after:

  • 1 January 1999 in England and Wales
  • 8 December 2014 in Scotland
  • 5 September 2022 in Northern Ireland

What to do next

If you've already applied to register the disposal of property or land with the relevant land registry, you need to wait until it has been removed from their records.

You can search the land registries online to check if the overseas entity is still listed on their records (they will charge a fee):

  • HM Land Registry (for England and Wales)
  • Land Register of Scotland
  • Land Registry for Northern Ireland

You may also need to file your overseas entity update statement if it is due.

Contact Companies House if you need to speak to someone.
