Apply to remove an overseas entity from the Register of Overseas Entities

If an overseas entity does not own any property or land in the UK, it can apply to be removed from the Register of Overseas Entities.

Once an overseas entity has been removed from the register, its Overseas Entity ID will no longer be valid. This means it will not be able to buy, sell, transfer, lease or charge its property or land in the UK.
