You cannot apply to de-register this overseas entity

An overseas entity cannot apply to be de-registered if it still appears on the Land Registry as the owner of any property or land in the UK.

You can check this by searching the Land Registry for the part of the UK the property or land is located in:

  • HM Land Registry (for England and Wales)
  • Land Registry for Northern Ireland
  • Land Register of Scotland

You may also need to file your overseas entity update statement if it is due.

Contact Companies House if you need to speak to someone.
