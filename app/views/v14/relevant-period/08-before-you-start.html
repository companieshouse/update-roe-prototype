New information needed as part of this update statement

You've told us that ASHCROFT LIMITED was the registered owner of UK property or land during the pre-registration period.

As part of this update statement, you'll need to tell us if the entity had any of the following during the pre-registration period, that it has not already told us about:

  • registrable beneficial owners
  • trusts involved in the entity
  • beneficiaries of a trust involved in the entity

This is a new legal requirement so that we can understand who was involved in the entities that owned UK property or land when the Economic Crime and Transparency Bill came into force (28 February 2022).

The pre-registration period for ASHCROFT LIMITED is between 28 February 2022 and 18 September 2022.
